The studio

A shed at the edge
of the trees.
Built from salvage.

The studio started as a project — something to build, something to use. Old boards, a found door, pieces from other things. It's where candles get poured, soap gets made, and crafts get dreamed up.

Not everything made here ends up for sale. Some of it is just the work of having hands that need to make things.

A note on availability

We sell what we have, when we have it.

This isn't a shop with consistent stock and a shipping department. Studio goods appear when time allows. The best way to find out what's available is to follow along on social or drop us a message.

We'll always be straight with you about what we have and when it's coming.
